Who We Are

Blue Baasa is a healthcare financing company that aims to improve access to healthcare at anytime and from anywhere. We empower financially undeserved people in rural and urban communities with innovative individual, family, and community financing solutions to overcome their financial barriers and pave the way for a healthier world.

How it works

Register for a token

Simple and straightforward access to financing. Call us or visit our office to register and receive a token representing your approved credit limit.
Use token with any merchant

Present your token at our wide network of partner clinics, hospitals, and pharmacies to receive care without delay. We handle the payment verification seamlessly.
Pay any time

Flexibility is key. Settle your bill through manageable installments. Repay via mobile money, bank transfers, or at our offices.
