The Power of MyCare Plus: The Community Health Fund

How the 80/20 Co-Pay Works

When a MyCare Plus member has an approved health cost, they don't face it alone. The community steps in to cover the vast majority of the bill.

This makes even large, unexpected bills manageable and ensures you can always afford the care you need.

Example: A 100,000 UGX Bill

The member is only responsible for their 20% co-payment of 20,000 UGX. The Community Fund covers the remaining 80,000 UGX.
